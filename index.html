FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Teams ask before adopting AI knowledge search and automations.

What is Alpha Minoris?

Alpha Minoris is an AI-powered knowledge search and automation platform. It helps teams find answers across company tools and automate repetitive work.

Will users see data they should not?

No. Results are permission-aware. People only see what they already have access to in your existing tools.

What tools can you connect?

Connect tools like Slack, Google Drive, Confluence, Jira, GitHub, Gmail, and Calendar. We bring the information together so your team can search in one place.

Is it GDPR compliant?

Yes. Alpha Minoris is designed for GDPR and data sovereignty. You control where data lives and how processing happens.

How long does it take to get value?

Most teams see value quickly once key tools are connected. Start with a few sources and expand over time as adoption grows.

What can the Smart Assistant do?

Assistant monitors email and chat, recognizes routine requests like scheduling or information sharing, and prepares one-click actions for users to approve.
